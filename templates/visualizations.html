ðŸ’¡ Key Insights

1. Data Imbalance Challenge

The healthy class has significantly fewer samples (152) compared to diseased classes (1000 each). This reflects real-world scenarios where diseased samples are more readily available for research. We use data augmentation and class weights to handle this imbalance.

2. Disease Characteristics

Early Blight creates dark spots with concentric rings, while Late Blight causes water-soaked lesions. The visual differences help the CNN model distinguish between them effectively. Transfer learning from ImageNet further improves feature extraction.

3. Real-World Impact

In African agriculture, small-scale farmers lose 30-50% of crops to diseases annually. Early detection through this AI system can reduce losses significantly, improve food security, and provide accessible diagnostic tools where agricultural experts are scarce.
